{Hack}

InternHacks is a hackathon focused on portfolio website development.
No teams, no worries.
Join us for a day-long hackathon founded by interns, for interns, hosted at the beautiful General Assembly office in San Francisco's financial district.

{Learn}

New to web development? Want to learn the basics of HTML/CSS, how to host a site, a new web framework, or how to build a site with Weebly?
Throughout the day, InternHacks will be holding workshops on various web dev topics, so you can brush up on the skills needed to make a great personal website.

{Network}

InternHacks is open to tech interns from throughout the Bay Area, and is a great way to get to know interns from other companies and universities.
