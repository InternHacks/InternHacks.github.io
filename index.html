{ FAQ }

What is InternHacks?

InternHacks is a different kind of hackathon. Focused on portfolio website development, no teams, no conflict-of-interest, no worries. Join us for a day-long workshop filled hackathon founded by interns, for interns.
What is a portfolio website?
A portfolio website is a lot like a resume, but showcased as a website.
It's a great way to display your technical skills to recruiters.
I don't know HTML/CSS, can I still attend?
Of course! Stop by the Intro to HTML/CSS and Weebly workshop to get started on your first porfolio website.

Where/When is InternHacks?

InternHacks will take place on Saturday, July 25th, from 9am-6pm
at the beautiful General Assembly office at 225 Bush Street 5th Floor, San Francisco.
